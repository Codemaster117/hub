How to use:

  1. Click "Start Recording" and allow microphone access when prompted
  2. Speak into your microphone - the timer will count down from 30 seconds
  3. The recording will stop automatically after 30 seconds, or you can stop manually
  4. Play back your recording using the audio player
  5. Download your recording to save it to your device

Works in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and other modern browsers
